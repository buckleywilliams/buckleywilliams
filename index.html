We are a tiny design studio specialising in prototyping, research & development, and communication design.

We help you and your organisation design and build software and connected products, decide what to build in the first place, and figure out how to communicate what you’re doing.

Our studio helps at every stage of a project: research, idea conception, initial prototyping and further development.

We write about our thinking and process, teach design students and run training workshops.

Our work involves a variety of technologies and organisations, from individual artists and arts organisations, through startups, to national newspapers. We have combined experience building connected devices, mobile applications, server-side software and tools, client-side applications and traditional websites.

Technologies we work with

iOS, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, Node and other JavaScript tools, Ruby (and Rails), Python, HTML, CSS
